Tiana

About Tiana

Tiana is a hardworking waitress, that dreams of opening her own restaurant in New Orleands one day. Her dream takes a slight detour when she meets Prince Naveen, who has been turned into a frog by an evil spell. Confusing Tiana for a princess and in hopes of breaking the spell, Naveen plants a kiss on Tiana -- thereby turning her into a frog as well. Together they go on an adventure through the bayou to seek the help of a powerful voodoo priestess.
